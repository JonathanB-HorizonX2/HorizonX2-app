Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2026

Thank you for using StarFlight. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

StarFlight does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

The app does not require user accounts and does not transmit any personal information to external servers.

2. App Functionality

StarFlight is a general aviation checklist app designed to assist pilots during flight operations. All checklist data and settings are stored locally on your device.

No analytics, tracking tools, or third-party advertising services are used.

3. Third-Party Services

StarFlight does not use third-party services that collect user data.

4. Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of external data exposure through the app.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if app functionality changes in the future. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
